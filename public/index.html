Morgan Schwartz

PhD Candidate in Biological Sciences

Caltech

Biography

I am a graduate student in the Van Valen Lab at Caltech. I am combining deep learning with molecular biology techniques in order to develop a method for creating spatial optical barcodes. My methodology will enable high throughput live cell library screens that will be cheaper and faster than existing techniques.

As an undergraduate, I worked with Michael Barresi at Smith College. I developed a method for quantitatively comparing embryonic zebrafish forebrains.
